About

I am a Ph.D. candidate at the Seoul National University focusing on Human-Computer Interaction and User Experience. My research interests lie in data-driven behavior analysis and user modeling. Currently, I am finishing up my thesis (expected graduation Aug. 2022) on identifying and collecting quantifiable behavior data to assist in understanding of user behaviors. As a UX researcher, I am passionate about using data-driven insights to understand users, improve products to support their needs, and ultimately create an efficient and elevated experience.

Outside of work, my hobbies include cooking, mobile filmmaking, and ultimate (flying disc sport), which I play competitively. I have had the priveledge to represent my country as a member of the Korea national team for various Asia-Oceania and Worlds events.
