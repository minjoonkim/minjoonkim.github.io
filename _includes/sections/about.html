About

I am a UX Researcher working for UniProt at EMBL-EBI, the European Bioinformatics Institue of the European Molecular Biology Laboratory. Prior to working at EMBL-EBI I was pursuing my Ph.D. at Seoul National University, focusing on data-driven behavior analysis and user modeling. As a UX researcher I take a mixed-methods approach to my work, combining quantitative data analysis with user interviews and insights. I am passionate about using data-driven insights to understand users, improve products to support their needs, and ultimately create an efficient and elevated experience.

Outside of work, my hobbies include coffee and ultimate (flying disc sport), which I play competitively. I have had the priveledge to represent my country as a member of the Korea national team for various Asia-Oceania and Worlds events.
